3D'omics Training Activities

7 April 2025

"Food System Innovations and Climate Change - Microbial Game-Changers" (Oslo, Norway)

The program of this one-day stakeholder seminar featured keynote lectures by prominent researchers and a panel discussion during which academic researchers presented the potential of microbiome/hologenomics to tackle major production challenges, and industry representatives presented their current work, needs, and interests. For more details about the event click here!

12-16 May 2025

"Spatial Microbiomics - From Sampling to Analysis" (Copenhagen, Denmark)

The course focused on the creation of a Microbiomics Atlas, including all working steps from sampling to analysis. The course was mainly aimed at PhD candidates and postdocs working on data generation, data processing, and data analysis in the field of microbiomics, but was open to other interested parties as well. The course also provided hands-on experience and helped to gain fundamental expertise to run this workflow. For more details about the event click here!

24-25 Sept 2025

"Data Integration with Microbial Networks and Community Models" (Leuven, Belgium)

Data integration is a challenging but necessary step to derive hypotheses and insights from meta-omics data of microbial communities. In this summer school, we want to introduce students at the Master and PhD level to black-box and white-box data integration methods, i.e. approaches that do not require a mechanistic understanding (e.g. association networks, prediction of species distributions with machine learning) and those that do (mechanistic models). For this, we will combine introductory talks with research talks on the latest developments in the field. 

For more details and REGISTRATION click here!
